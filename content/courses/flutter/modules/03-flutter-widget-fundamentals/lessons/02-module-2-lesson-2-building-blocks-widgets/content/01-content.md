title: "What Are Widgets?"
---

Widgets are the fundamental building blocks of Flutter applications. Everything you see on screen in a Flutter app is a widget - from the app bar at the top, to buttons, text, images, and even the layout structures that hold them all together.

Think of widgets like LEGO bricks. Just as you can combine LEGO pieces to build anything from a simple house to a complex spaceship, you combine widgets to create any user interface you can imagine. Each widget has a specific purpose and can be customized with properties to change its appearance and behavior.

In this lesson, we'll explore the two main types of widgets (stateless and stateful), learn how to compose widgets together, and understand the widget tree - the hierarchy that Flutter uses to render your app on screen.
